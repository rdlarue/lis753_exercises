about

a portrait of the artist

Rebekkah [Bee] LaRue (she/her) recieved her BFA in Studio Art from Cazenovia College in 2019. She won the award for Best Capstone on her project Love, Grandpa.

Originally from Upstate NY, Bee now lives and opperates out of Chicago. She is currently in pursuit of her Master in Library and Information Science. She loves art but is willing to sell a part of her soul to have a job with health insurance.

When she is not drawing or doing homework, Bee likes to watch hockey, listen to Dungeons and Dragons podcasts, and play with her cat, Vincent.
